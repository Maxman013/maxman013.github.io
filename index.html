Maximum Bots


Hi! My name is Max and this is a website that holds information about the bots I've created.

A bot is a program that automatically posts to Facebook pages on a schedule, often with randomised content.

I have created 4 bots, each of which has a summary below. I have also created an "Access Token Tool" that allows other bot creators to easily implement automatic posting.


PaintBot

PaintBot is a bot that posts randomly generated canvas images. The images it generates can contain up to 10 shapes with random colours in random locations. This was the first bot I made in March 2018 and now has over 7000 likes.


LossBot 12250

LossBot 12250 is a bot that posts randomly generated images, in an attempt to recreate a minimalist version of the "Loss" meme. The images it generates contain four panels, with random lines drawn in them. This was the second bot I made in July 2018 and now has over 3000 likes. I made this in collaboration with another person.


CountryBot 0208

CountryBot 0208 is a bot that posts randomly generated strings. The strings it generates use the letters from one country, and is the length of another. This was the third bot I also made in July 2018 and now has over 2500 likes.


CommentBot 2005

CommentBot 2005

CommentBot 2005 is a bot that posts random comments from random YouTube videos. This was the fourth bot I made in September 2018 and now has almost 4000 likes.
